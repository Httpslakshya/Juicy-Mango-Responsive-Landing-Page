VARIETIES

Alphonso

Alphonso mango is also known as Hapus mango. It has a rich sweet taste with complex aromatic notes.

Kesar

Kesar mangoes are also known as kesari. They are saffron in colour. It is known for its unique sweet taste and frequent and distinctive flavour.

Dasheri

A Dasheri Mango are known for their sweetness & more pulp. It is a medium - large-sized mango with small seeds. It has yellow skin with light red or orange spots.

Totapuri

Totapuri mangoes have a unique taste that is less sweet as compared to Alphonso and Kesar. It has a tangy taste with a slight sourness

Langra

Langra mango is very popular among the Indians It has fewer fibres and a smooth texture which tastes sweet and mildly tart.

Chausa

Chausa mangoes are highly popular in South Asia. They have a sweet honey-like flavour with a blend of slightly acidity.
